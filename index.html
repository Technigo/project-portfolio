a bit about myself

so,

I am Åsa, a creative strategist and a junior UX- & product designer. I love everything that has to do with design; graphic- and digital as well as contemporary art. And I'm totally spellbound by the way design, as a method, has opened up a new level of creativity for me, nevertheless opens up a new world to the future of creativity. I love the instant aesthetic result of the creative process; from sketching & building structure to adding form & colour. It's my firm belief that design can make a difference and as a designer & developer I hold and manage the huge responsibility that comes with the power. I design from a source of curiosity and empathy, with a combination of deep strategic thinking, bold ideas and artistic craftsmanship. But, I'm not all business. Firstly, I would like to see myself as an optimistic and kind person who really values the importance of creating a great workplace culture where people can shine, flourish and most of all - have fun. My goal is to create lifelong learning. I think it's time to take action: stop postponing happiness for the future and design it for the present. Design - and let it emerge from affinities, integration & love.
