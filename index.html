Welcome! My name's Nick, and I'm a frontend developer with a background in writing and localisation. I'm passionate about problem-solving, language and explaining complex information in a simple way. As a technical translator, I spent nearly a decade breaking down and understanding complicated ideas and processes in one language and translating them into another. I now work with programming languages in a similar fashion.

TECH

I've also worked with responsive (mobile-first) design, web accessibility, APIs, agile development, mob programming, pair programming and code reviews.
