Hello! I’m a graphic designer who decided to go digital by diving into the world of web development. I studied design and visual communication but I’ve always had a technical side as well and taking the step into programming lets me finally and truly combine them. What I most of all bring from my +10 years as a designer into frontend development I would say is great attention to detail, in aesthetics and code alike.
