This is me

In August, I started the Technigos 24 weeks Frontend Bootcamp. An extremely intense period with peaks and valleys to say the least. I have worked closely with Frontend Developers for many years as a Designer and have more and more felt that this is something I would like to master. So glad I jumped on this trip. It is an eternal learning. But I did not think that I would get this far in 24 weeks. Looking forward to implementing this knowledge in my role as Visual Designer.
